My top 3 movies of all-time

Schiendler's List

This is one of the best movies of all time and probably the best movie of Steven Spielberg. It showcases the horrors of the war and especially the life of eople in Auschwitz camp. The most important thing that we can learn from this film is that we should be happy with whatever we have and not take things for granted. We should remember that our necessity might be someone's luxury. So we should be happy and grateful with the way we lead out life.

Pather Panchali

Pather Panchali is a film directed by Satyajit Ray and it was his directorial debut. The film is about simple village life where there lives a family.The film revolves around various events that occured surrounding the members of the family and the emotions that they felt. The film is about life and viewers can feel a variety of emotions while watching the film. Also the way Satyajit Ray showed emotions in this film is really a treat to watch. It is a must watch for everyone.

The Godfather

The Godfather is one of the best movies of all time. Directed by Francis Ford Coppola, this movie revolves around gang war. Marlon Brando plays Don Corleone who is the main character of the movie. The film also features Al Pacino who plays son of Don Corleone, Michael, and Diane Keaton, girlfriend of Michael. Brando was fabulous with his acting and he is much appreciated for this role. It is a drama film and people have enjoyed watching it over the years.
